Blade and Soul

Feb 16, 2016

I have recently picked up Blade and Soul as a game that I enjoy playing during my down time aside from school and work. Blade and Soul is another MMORPG that features both PvP and PvE.
The storyline to Blade and Soul is well written and keeps you moving along to find out what happens next. So far, the max level is 45 and the main story line is not yet complete (as of this post).
You can find people streaming this game on Twitch.tv or by visiting BladeAndSoul. Although, most people playing the game find more interest in PvP, so you may not find the game overall appealing until you stumble upon a streamer that is also enjoying the PvE aspect (unless, of course, you enjoy PvP).

If you would like to tag along in this adventure, I am currently on Onmyung server under character name Vectoria .
