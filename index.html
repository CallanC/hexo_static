Spare Time

2016-02-16
Video Games / SanctumGP / Entertainment

During school or free time at home, I find myself doing a variety of things.
Aside from searching for different blog softwares, I am always on the lookout for other things to do or watch, such as YouTube videos, Twitch, or even Video Games.

On Twitch…

On Twitch, I watch mainly three streamers. I prefer watching MarkeeDragon from time to time.
MarkeeDragon mainly streams EVE-Online and has discounts for EVE-Online to start your online experience off a little better.

When MarkeeDragon is not streaming, I am also watching and supporting imaqtpie imaqtpie who mainly streams League of Legends.
He has some neat commentary and is overall interesting to watch as well as the plays he is able to pull through at times.

At night, around 11PM Mountain Standard Time, I end up watching LastFairDeal Patrick, or “LastFairDeal”, on Twitch if the night is slow. His commentary on the video games he plays and how he reacts to situations can often be humurous.
He is open minded on his chat and allows jokes to be posted as long as they are not hurtful towards anyone.

Video Games…

During more slow nights or mornings, I enjoy playing a few video games. On the off chances, I enjoy playing League of Legends with friends from SanctumGP who can be found on TeamSpeak at ts.sanctumgp.com.
However, there are times where League of Legends is not all that entertaining and another game that I have been enjoying playing is BladeandSoul.
